Penne Alla Vodka

Description

Penne alla vodka is fantastic Italian dish, consisting of a flavorful sauce augmented by vodka, tomatoes, and garlic, simmered over heat. Penne pasta does a wonderful job of carrying the sauce, which is the focal point of this dish. A true classic!

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces of penne pasta
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 can of plum tomatoes, drained and chopped
  • 1/4 cup of vodka
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (don't skip!)
  • 1/2 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup of shredded Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil.
  2. Cook the pasta according to it's instructions or to your taste.
  3. While the pasta cooks, in a large pan/skillet, heat the butter and add the garlic. Cook this for around a minute on medium heat.
  4. Stir in the tomatoes, vodka, salt, and red pepper flakes.
  5. Bring this glorious, delicious stew of ingredients to a simmer and reduce heat.
  6. Allow this to simmer for around five minutes, then stir in the cream.
  7. Cook for another couple minutes, tasting every minute.
  8. Once proper flavor is reached, remove from heat.
  9. Drain pasta once it's ready.
  10. Add pasta and cheese to the sauce pan/skillet. Stir/toss to combine.
  11. Enjoy! :)
